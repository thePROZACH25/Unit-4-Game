Snake Attack!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four snakes below. By clicking on a snake you will be adding a specific aount of points to you total score. You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if you total score goes above the random number. The value of each snake is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each snakes.
